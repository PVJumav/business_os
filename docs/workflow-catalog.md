# Workflow Catalog

## Seeded Workflow Templates

1. Hire-to-Retire
   - Workforce planning, recruitment, offer, contract signing, employee creation, account provisioning, asset allocation, payroll activation, KPI assignment, training, performance, offboarding, account deactivation, final settlement.

2. Lead-to-Cash
   - Marketing campaign, lead creation, lead scoring, opportunity qualification, presales, solution design, quotation, margin validation, discount approval, contract review, customer conversion, project creation, procurement, implementation, invoice, payment, revenue recognition, SLA activation, renewal.

3. Procure-to-Pay
   - Purchase request, manager approval, budget validation, vendor validation, legal review, purchase order, delivery, inspection, invoice receipt, three-way matching, payment approval, vendor payment, audit closure.

4. Incident-to-Resolution
   - Incident detection, ticket creation, classification, SLA assignment, engineer assignment, escalation, resolution, customer confirmation, RCA, corrective action, closure.

5. Project Implementation & SLA
   - Contract signed, project creation, charter, resource allocation, task planning, budget allocation, procurement, implementation, milestone review, UAT, signoff, go-live, SLA activation, health checks, renewal tracking.

6. Contract Lifecycle
   - Contract request, template selection, drafting, legal review, risk assessment, finance review, management approval, e-signature, archive, renewal reminder, amendment or closure.

7. Enterprise KPI Reporting
   - Data collection, validation, KPI calculation, department review, executive dashboard, variance analysis, corrective action assignment, progress monitoring.

## Workflow States

Draft, Submitted, Under Review, Approved, Rejected, Executing, Waiting for External Action, Escalated, Completed, Closed, Cancelled.
